Starfish

Starfish feeds by extending their stomach out of its mouth and over the prey.
  • Scientific name: Asteroidea
  • Average Length:10 centimeters
  • Average Life Span: 35 years
  • Habitat:Tropical coral reefs, rocky shores, tidal pools, mud, and sand to kelp forests, seagrass meadows and the deep-sea floor down to at least 6,000 m
Statfish is a marine invertebrate having a central disc and five arms brightly coloured in various shades. Most can regenerate damaged parts or lost arms and they can shed arms as a means of defence.
